AdMotion
AI-Powered Smart Vehicle Advertising Platform
Live Admin Dashboard
AI Scheduling Engine
AdMotion turns the unused roof space on taxis & commercial vehicles into smart moving digital billboards. An AI engine decides which ad runs on which vehicle, where and when — using GPS location, time of day & live weather to maximise real-world reach.
AI FEATURES
🤖
AI Ad Scheduling & Optimization
Picks the best ads per vehicle by GPS area, time-slot, weather & fairness scoring.
📈
AI Reach-Estimation Engine
Estimates real-world impressions per play (area × time × weather).
🧠
ML Impression Prediction
scikit-learn model learns from collected data to predict reach & demand.
🎯
Smart Geo & Weather Targeting
Geo-fenced city/area detection auto-targets the right audience.
🎙️
AI Voice-Command Control
Hands-free dashboard control via real-time speech recognition.
⚙️
Self-Optimizing Budget Pacing
AI balances spend across the fleet so every campaign's budget is used efficiently.
